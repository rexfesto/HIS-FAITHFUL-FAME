Our Vision

To build up the Christian faith and extend the love of Christ through practical acts of charity, in fulfillment of the scriptures:

"Charity suffereth long" – 1 Corinthians 13
"The greatest of these is love" – 1 Corinthians 13

Our Mission

To share the gospel not only in words but also in action — meeting the needs of individuals, communities, families, and transforming environments for Christ.

Our Objectives

  • Support Christian and individuals in need through practical charity.
  • Encourage believers to grow in faith and love.
  • Provide practical aid assistance in key areas of life.
  • Mobilize partners and branches of the ministry to advance the gospel through love.

Our Operations

Elect Trust Endowment has been active since 2013 and continues as a quarterly structure, focusing on different groups and outreach areas.

  • Student Outreach – support for Bible school students and seminarians.
  • Crusades & Evangelism – spreading the gospel in communities.
  • Prison Outreach – helping detainees establish social businesses.
  • Outreach to the Homeless & Vulnerable – providing food and encouragement.

Note: Beneficiaries may receive varying levels of support (cash, food, or materials) depending on available resources.

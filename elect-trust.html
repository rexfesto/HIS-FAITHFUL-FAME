ELECT TRUST ENDOWMENT

(Agbani Dean Kipe Initiative)

Currently Ongoing

Our Vision

To build up the Christian faith and extend the love of Christ through practical acts of charity, in fulfillment of the scriptures:

“Charity edifies” — 1 Corinthians 8:1
“The greatest of these is love” — 1 Corinthians 13

Our Mission

To share the gospel not only in words but also in action — meeting the needs of individuals, strengthening believers, and transforming communities for Christ.

Our Objectives

  • Support Christians and individuals in need through practical charity.
  • Encourage believers to grow in faith and love.
  • Provide partial or full assistance in key areas of life, as resources allow.
  • Mobilize partners and friends of the ministry to advance the gospel through love.

Our Operations

Elect Trust Endowment has been active since 2015 and operates on a quarterly structure, focusing on different groups and outreach areas:

  • Students’ Quarter – support for Bible school students and seminaries.
  • Crusades & Evangelism – spreading the gospel in communities.
  • Business Start-ups – helping Christians establish small businesses.
  • Outreach to the Homeless & Vulnerable – providing relief and encouragement.

Note: Beneficiaries may receive varying levels of support (30%, 50%, or more), depending on available resources.
